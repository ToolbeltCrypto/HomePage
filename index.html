TOKEN

Overview:

Solutions focused token with community ownership and revenue generating dapps.

Tokenomics:

Transfer fees: 4% into staking.

Total supply: 1M tokens.

Supply distribution: 10% to team, 90% to open-market.

Minting: Only at token deployment.

Alterable:

------------------------------------------------

Change token transfer fee.

setTaxPerThousand(uint)

Requirements: <= 100 (10%).

------------------------------------------------

Exclude transfer fee with address.

addIgnoreFee(address)

------------------------------------------------

Include transfer fee with address.

removeIgnoreFee(address)

------------------------------------------------

LOCKER

Overview:

Lock smart contract and restore community ownership.

Solution:

Most developers have exclusive access to certain functions. Posing a centralization risk, this allows for contract exploitation.

The locker secures a dimension of trust by granting community access to all contract changes.

Voting:

75%+ to successfully change the smart contract.

Since inactive members are more likely to resist change, voting is not based on 50%.

Vote to enable/disable voting.

Fees:

Resets days after last creation.

weiBaseFee * 2^(creationAmount)

Alterable:

------------------------------------------------

Voting duration.

setVoteEndPeriod(uint)

Requirements: between 86400 and 2592000 seconds.

------------------------------------------------

Fee expansion duration since last creation.

setFeeResetPeriod(uint)

Requirements: between 86400 and 2592000 seconds.

------------------------------------------------

Create Delta cost multiplier.

setWeiBaseFee(uint)

Requirements: between 10^10 and 10^20 Toolbelt tokens in wei.

------------------------------------------------

Stakers' voting power multiplier.

setStakeVotePercent(uint)

Requirements: <= 10000%.

------------------------------------------------

AFFILIATE

Overview:

Refer investors and sign up with an affiliate to earn and discount transfer fees, respectively.

Transfer portions (Toolbelt):

80% of fee to staking.

15% of fee to affiliate.

5% discount to sender.

Transfer portions (other):

5% of fee to staking.

PRESALE

Overview:

Particpate in the creation of a token's liquidity pool.

Liquidity soft locks for a year.

Solution:

Most presales require payment to initiate the presale. This barrier inhibits the growth of new projects.

To combat this, Toolbelt has created a payment free presale avaliable to any token.

Lock agreement:

Unlocking liquidity causes a penalty based on time left. The entire fee will be sent to stakers.

Toolbelt is unable to unlock liquidity prior to a year.

EVENT

Overview:

Subscribe to any blockchain event and keep track of its information.

What are events?

Token events are ways to observe blockchain interactions.

For example, an event is emitted during a token transfer. Etherscan tracks transfer events and displays them in a viewable format.

Cost:

Stake one token per subscription.

Unstaking will not remove subscriptions.

Unsubscribing will not reduce the stake requirement.

APPROVAL

Overview:

Track and alter token approvals accordingly.

What are approvals?

Token approvals allow contracts to take your tokens.

For example, a swap takes your tokens, converts them into different tokens, then transfers the other tokens back.

Cost:

Free for anyone to use.

STAKING

Overview:

Lock tokens to generate revenue.

Yield varies based on transfer fees and dapp adoption.

Lock agreement:

Claim rewards at anytime.

Unstaking prior to a year will result in a fee awarded to stakers.
